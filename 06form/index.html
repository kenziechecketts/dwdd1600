What is Pickleball?

  • Pickleball is a sport in which you use a paddle to hit a wiffle ball which is a hollow plastic ball that is perforated with holes. It has rapidly become a very popular sport over these past couple of years especially in the U.S.
  • Pickleball is a 2 to 4 player sport, just like tennis you have the option of playing doubles or singles. In fact, Pickleball is a combination of and uses elements from tennis, table tennis, and badminton.

The History of Pickleball

Who invented Pickleball?
Pickleball was invented by a group of 3 men; Joel Pritchard, Bill Bell, and Barney McCallum
How was it invented?
The sport of Pickleball was created by mere accident. One day Pritchard, Bell, and McCallum and their families had gathered together and they had quickly become bored. They rememebered that they had a badminton court however they soon discovered that they didn't have enough badminton rackets, so they resorted to using ping pong paddles. They also discovered that they didn't have a badminton birdie so they resorted to a wiffle ball. This makeshift game soon evolved into a competitive and well known sport known as Pickleball.
Why is it called Pickleball?
Pritchard decided to name it Pickleball because he was trying to reference to a "pickle boat". A Pickle boat is a term associated with crew boat racing. The Pickle boat was referred to as the boat that finished last in the race. This makes sense why this inspired the name Pickleball because it is a farely new sport and "last in the race".

How to get started

Pickleball is a sport for anyone and everyone. There are no prior skills or knowledge that is required in order to play.
All you need is...

  1. A Paddle
  2. A Ball
  3. And A Court

Let's not forget that you will also need a friend or two to bring along!
